Bootstrap This node may or may not be the right place to write the data. StorageProxy's job is to get the nodes (all the replicas) that are responsible for holding the data that is going to be written. It utilizes a replication strategy to do this. Once the replica nodes are identified, it sends the RowMutation message to them, the node waits for replies from these nodes, but it does not wait for all the replies to come. It only waits for as many responses as are enough to satisfy the client's minimum number of successful writes defined by ConsistencyLevel.
